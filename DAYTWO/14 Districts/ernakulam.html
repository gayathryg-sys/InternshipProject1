Ernakulam-Commercial Capital of Kerala

Ernakulam, often referred to as the "Queen of the Arabian Sea," is a vibrant district in Kerala, India, known for its bustling commercial and cultural scene. It forms the urban part of Kochi city and is a major hub for trade, finance, and industry. The district boasts a rich history, a blend of traditional and modern architecture, and is a gateway to the scenic Kerala backwaters. Ernakulam is also recognized for its high literacy rate and is a popular destination for both business and leisure travelers.
